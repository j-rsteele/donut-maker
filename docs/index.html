Inspiration for the game

This game was inspired by the original Cookie Clicker Game

To play click the bake button until you reach 100 donuts to activate the auto click button. When you've earned enough donuts you can purchase auto clickers and multipliers. Auto clickers will automatically click for us every second, so we don't have to click manually, and donut multipliers increase the number of donuts made for each click.
